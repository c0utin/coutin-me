Law Hunter: Platform to Achieve Legal Research and NLP Model for Search -> Bank Of America

Law Hunter is an platform designed to facilitate legal research by leveraging advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) models. It enables users to quickly search and retrieve relevant legal documents, laws, and case precedents using a powerful search algorithm. The platform improves legal professionals' efficiency by providing precise and contextual results tailored to the legal field. Additionally, Law Hunter incorporates a tree-based tagging model similar to PageRank, enhancing the ranking of search results by evaluating the relevance and importance of legal documents in a structured way.

Asimov: Develop a Method to Predict Defaults in FIDC Funds -> Comissão de Valores Imobiliários (CVM)

Asimov has undertaken a project aimed at developing a method to predict defaults in FIDC (Funds for the Acquisition of Credit Rights), enhancing proactive measures to mitigate risks. This initiative, in collaboration with the Comissão de Valores Imobiliários (CVM), focuses on creating a predictive model that analyzes various financial indicators and historical data to forecast potential defaults. By identifying at-risk funds early, stakeholders can implement preventive strategies, thereby safeguarding investments and promoting greater stability within the financial market. This proactive approach not only benefits investors but also contributes to a more resilient and transparent financial ecosystem.
